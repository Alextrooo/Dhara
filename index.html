About Dhara

Dhara is an open source, hybrid parallel, and integrated framework for high-resolution ecohydrologic and biogeochemical modeling.

Multi-layer approach

Implements multi-layer canopy approach to resolve the vertical profiles within the canopy

Hybrid parallel computing

Combines the best of MPI and CUDA worlds to overcome computational challenges.

Optimized for extreme scales

Utilize emerging high-resolution topographic data for large-scale simulations.

Microtopography

Capture and incorporate micro-topographic variability and controls on landscape for modeling.
